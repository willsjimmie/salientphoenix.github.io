About us

BUSINESS RUN WITH OVER 60 YEARS IN THE BUILDING INDUSTRY, OVER 200 STAFF MEMBERS AND A TURNOVER OF OVER £43M.

The business was created in 1952 by Ailbeart Morven as a sales of building materials and in 1962 the business grew into construction company and has continued to grow year on year, now directly employing over 200 personnel. That is, the quality you get for the price is the value you get for your money. The cheapest price is not necessarily, the best value, if the quality is unsatisfactory. This equation is nowhere more important than when building. The home is the biggest investment most people make in their lives, and building a home (or business premises) ranks among the highest stress producing circumstances a person can experience. This is why SPC Built developed our own unique approach of applied perfectionism to take the brunt of the millions of issues involved in building onto ourselves, so our clients can enjoy peace of mind and experience the positive joys of creating something beautiful.Showcasing a portfolio of new homes, alterations and additions in the eastern suburbs, lower north shore and inner west, our team of trusted quality tradesmen and contractors are proven craftsmen who have assisted us in achieving award-winning results. SPC Built pride themselves on maintaining constant clear and helpful communication through all stages of construction, between all parties involved, and overseeing every phase personally. We strive to make your home building experience pleasant by conducting ourselves in a professional, approachable, agreeable, courteous, and easy-going manner.
